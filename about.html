About GRAND HYATT Hotel

Luxury and elegance at the heart of Bangkok

Our Story

Grand Hyatt Bangkok offers an unparalleled experience of luxury and comfort in the heart of Thailand’s vibrant capital. Every detail of our hotel reflects a commitment to excellence, blending modern elegance with authentic Thai hospitality.

We take pride in being a premier destination for travelers seeking sophistication and relaxation. From our world-class amenities to our exceptional service, your stay promises to be an unforgettable journey in the heart of Bangkok.

Prime Location in Bangkok

Our hotel is nestled in the heart of Bangkok's vibrant Charoen Krung Road area, near the Chao Phraya River. This location offers the perfect blend of urban convenience and accessibility to the city's top attractions, business districts, and cultural landmarks.
